Expert Info

Wireshark detects specific states of protocols to help analysts easily spot possible anomalies and problems.

Expert info can provide a group of categories in three different severities. Details are shown in the table below.

<img width="869" height="194" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/be0a43d8-4b36-4154-a654-8ef4860d538e" />

You can use the “lower left bottom section” in the status bar or “Analyse → Expert Information” menu to view all available information entries via a dialogue box. It will show the packet number, summary, group protocol and total occurrence.
